## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

[err-00002] helpful=0 harmful=1 :: Do not confuse: • Shares issued in an acquisition with shares sold in a market transaction. • Gross cash paid for an acquisition with cash paid net of cash acquired. • Total purchase‑price consideration with the cash component of that consideration.
[err-00006] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not let the word "sold" automatically dictate a sale‑related tag for convertible securities. Mandatory convertible preferred stock is issued by the company as financing; the term "sold" refers to the issuance, not a secondary‑market transaction. Misreading this leads to selecting SaleOfStock tags instead of issuance and conversion‑price tags.
[err-00010] helpful=1 harmful=0 :: Do not assume the numeric amount mentioned with a revolving credit facility refers to the remaining or available borrowing capacity unless the narrative explicitly uses qualifiers such as "available", "remaining", or provides a specific remaining figure. In the absence of such qualifiers, treat the figure as the facility’s total (maximum borrowing capacity) and use the LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity tag.
[err-00021] helpful=1 harmful=0 :: Do not assign different tags to entities that are semantically identical or belong to the same domain. Using multiple tags for the same concept creates inconsistency and reduces answer accuracy.
[err-00027] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Avoid selecting an overly specific tag when the narrative is generic. Align the tag’s specificity with the description: use generic tags (e.g., DebtInstrumentFairValue, LeaseAndRentalExpense) unless the text explicitly includes qualifiers such as "long‑term" or "operating lease".
[err-00037] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not confuse authorized share counts with shares outstanding or shares issued. Selecting ShareOutstanding or Issued tags for an authorized share figure leads to inaccurate tagging.
[err-00044] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assume a plain revolving credit facility amount represents the maximum borrowing capacity. Default to LineOfCreditFacilityCurrentBorrowingCapacity unless the text explicitly includes words like "maximum", "total facility amount", or "unused capacity".

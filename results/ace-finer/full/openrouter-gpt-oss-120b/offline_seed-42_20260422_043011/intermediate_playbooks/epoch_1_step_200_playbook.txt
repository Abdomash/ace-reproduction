[err-00001] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a share count mentioned in an acquisition context to CommonStockSharesOutstanding; that tag refers to the company’s total outstanding shares. Use BusinessAcquisitionEquityInterestsIssuedOrIssuableNumberOfSharesIssued for shares issued or issuable as part of a business acquisition.
[err-00002] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assume "aggregate purchase price" always means total consideration (cash + debt + equity). When the surrounding text describes a cash payment for the acquisition, the correct tag is PaymentsToAcquireBusinessesGross.
[err-00006] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map numbers related to mandatory convertible preferred securities to stock‑sale tags (e.g., SaleOfStockPricePerShare). Such figures represent a conversion price of a convertible debt instrument and should be tagged with DebtInstrumentConvertibleConversionPrice1. Likewise, avoid using SaleOfStockNumberOfSharesIssuedInTransaction for new share issuances; instead use StockIssuedDuringPeriodSharesNewIssues.
[err-00009] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not interpret the phrase "availability may be less than $X" as indicating the current amount available; the $X still represents the facility’s total limit and should be tagged with LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity. Use LineOfCreditFacilityCurrentBorrowingCapacity only when the text explicitly states the amount presently available to borrow.
[err-00013] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a stated interest rate (e.g., "8% interest rate") to the generic DebtInstrumentInterestRate tag. Use the more specific DebtInstrumentInterestRateStatedPercentage to capture the percentage nature of the rate.
[err-00015] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign StockRepurchaseProgram tags (e.g., StockRepurchaseProgramRemainingAuthorizedRepurchaseAmount1 or StockRepurchaseProgramAuthorizedAmount1) to numeric entities that appear in sentences describing a Long Term Incentive Plan, Equity Incentive Plan, or any other share‑based compensation arrangement. Those numbers belong to ShareBasedCompensationArrangement tags, not repurchase‑program tags.
[err-00018] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map expressions like "LIBOR plus X%" or "SOFR + Y%" to DebtInstrumentInterestRateStatedPercentage. These describe a spread over a variable reference rate and should be tagged with DebtInstrumentBasisSpreadOnVariableRate1.
[err-00020] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a borrowing‑base figure to LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity. The borrowing base reflects the current borrowing capacity and should be tagged LineOfCreditFacilityCurrentBorrowingCapacity.
[err-00023] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign a DebtInstrumentFaceAmount tag to an amount that is explicitly described as fair value, and do not assign DebtInstrumentFairValue to a principal/face amount. Mis‑identifying the role leads to inaccurate tagging, especially when both figures appear together in the same disclosure.
[err-00038] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not default to generic vesting‑period tags (e.g., ShareBasedCompensationArrangementByShareBasedPaymentAwardVestingPeriod1) when the text refers to a weighted‑average recognition period for unrecognized compensation cost. The taxonomy provides dedicated 'EmployeeServiceShareBasedCompensationNonvestedAwards...' tags; failing to search for them leads to incorrect UNKNOWN placeholders.
[err-00052] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a plain mention of a revolving credit facility amount (e.g., "a $400 million revolving credit facility") to LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity; use LineOfCreditFacilityCurrentBorrowingCapacity unless the text explicitly states it is a limit or ceiling.
[err-00068] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not default to the UNKNOWN placeholder simply because a suitable tag is not immediately obvious. First conduct a comprehensive keyword search—including partial matches—across the entire taxonomy to confirm that no appropriate tag exists. Only apply UNKNOWN after this exhaustive verification.
[err-00071] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a stated annual or periodic option grant limit to the NumberOfSharesAvailableForGrant tag; that tag represents the overall grant pool, whereas the period‑specific grant quantity should be tagged with OptionsGrantsInPeriodGross.
[err-00076] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map a duration that refers to an option's overall term to a vesting‑period tag (ShareBasedCompensationArrangementByShareBasedPaymentAwardAwardVestingPeriod1). Use the expiration‑period tag instead, and always verify the exact tag spelling and case as listed in the taxonomy.
[err-00084] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map installment or scheduled payments to PaymentsToAcquireBusinessesGross unless the disclosure explicitly states the payments are for acquiring a business. When the context describes regular payments to a lender or counterparty as debt service, use RepaymentsOfDebt.
[err-00088] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map share counts expressed with "up to", "maximum of", or similar ceiling language to ShareBasedCompensationArrangementByShareBasedPaymentAwardNumberOfSharesAvailableForGrant; those phrases refer to the authorized share limit, not the pool currently available for grant.
[err-00120] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not default to the 'UNKNOWN' placeholder if a suitable tag exists in the taxonomy (e.g., AreaOfRealEstateProperty for square‑footage values). Perform a thorough keyword‑to‑tag search before using UNKNOWN.
[err-00128] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map amounts described as "borrowings under the ... facility" to borrowing‑capacity tags. These figures represent debt already incurred and should be tagged as LongTermDebt (or the appropriate debt instrument tag) instead of LineOfCreditFacilityCurrentBorrowingCapacity or LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity.
[err-00138] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not tag an acquisition‑related ownership percentage as MinorityInterestOwnershipPercentageByParent; the correct tag for ownership acquired in a business combination is BusinessAcquisitionPercentageOfVotingInterestsAcquired.
[err-00156] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not reference internal bullet IDs that are not part of the official playbook; rely only on the textual cues and the provided taxonomy tags when selecting US GAAP tags.
[err-00161] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign OperatingLeasesRentExpenseNet when the disclosure only mentions rent or lease expense without specifying the lease type; default to the generic LeaseAndRentalExpense tag unless the text explicitly references an operating lease.
[err-00180] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not add acquisition qualifiers (e.g., "Acquired") to a tag unless the source text explicitly states that the asset or metric pertains to an acquisition. Selecting an acquisition‑specific tag for a generic description (such as a useful‑life period) leads to mismatches with the ground‑truth taxonomy.
[err-00182] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map unamortized debt issuance costs to InterestExpenseDebt; they represent financing costs that should be amortized and therefore belong to the AmortizationOfFinancingCosts tag.
[err-00194] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign the same US GAAP tag to both a principal amount and a fair‑value amount when they are mentioned together for the same debt instrument. Principal figures must use DebtInstrumentFaceAmount, while fair‑value figures must use LongTermDebtFairValue. Mixing or swapping these tags leads to incorrect tagging and mismatches with ground‑truth.
[err-00196] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign LongTermDebt (or ShortTermDebt) to an amount that is explicitly described as the outstanding or current balance of a particular debt instrument such as commercial paper, a loan, or notes payable. The correct tag for these instrument‑specific balances is DebtInstrumentCarryingAmount.
[err-00203] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not apply the sai-00051 rule (treating any revolving credit facility amount as current borrowing capacity). A plain mention of a revolving credit facility size should be tagged as LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity unless the text explicitly indicates it is the amount presently available to draw.
[err-00207] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign the GrantsInPeriod tag to amounts described as the aggregate grant‑date fair value of performance share unit awards issued in a period. The correct tag is the VestedInPeriodTotalFairValue tag, because the phrasing indicates a vested‑in‑period fair‑value measurement.
[err-00210] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assume that a percentage in one question determines the tag for a percentage in another question; always evaluate each numeric entity within its own question context, even if the same tag was used previously.
[err-00217] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map per‑share price expressions to share‑based compensation fair‑value tags. Treat them as SharePrice; only use share‑based compensation fair‑value tags when the text mentions grant‑date fair value of awards.
[err-00224] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign the generic loss‑contingency tag (LossContingencyAccrualAtCarryingValue) when the disclosure explicitly references an environmental loss contingency. Using the generic tag in such cases leads to mismatches; the correct tag is AccrualForEnvironmentalLossContingencies.
[err-00226] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assume an error exists when the predicted tags already satisfy all verification criteria; avoid making unnecessary changes that could introduce mistakes.
[err-00238] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not map phrases like "available for issuance" or "share ceiling" to ShareBasedCompensationArrangementByShareBasedPaymentAwardNumberOfSharesAvailableForGrant; these expressions denote the total authorized share pool, not the currently grantable pool.
[err-00266] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assume that longer tag variants (e.g., adding 'ShareBasedAwardsOtherThanOptions') exist in the taxonomy. Always check the official tag list for the exact tag name; using a non‑existent tag leads to mismatches with ground‑truth.
[err-00291] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign DebtInstrumentFaceAmount to amounts that represent credit‑facility limits or term‑loan limits within an amended facility; these should be tagged as LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity.
[err-00310] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Do not assign MinorityInterestOwnershipPercentageByParent to a percentage that describes voting interests acquired in a business combination. The correct tag for such percentages is BusinessAcquisitionPercentageOfVotingInterestsAcquired.

[ctx-00004] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Key lexical cues: • "issued", "issued as part of the acquisition", "equity interests issued" → share‑issuance tag. • "cash paid", "cash consideration", "aggregate purchase price" (when described as cash) → PaymentsToAcquireBusinessesGross. • "total consideration", "aggregate consideration", "including debt or equity" → BusinessCombinationConsiderationTransferred1.

Recognize that a conversion price pertains to a debt instrument, not a stock sale.
[ctx-00008] helpful=0 harmful=0 :: Key lexical cues for credit‑facility limits and spread percentages: • "maximum borrowing capacity", "revolving credit facility", "commitment amount", "facility limit" → use LineOfCreditFacilityMaximumBorrowingCapacity. • "margin", "basis spread", "spread on variable‑rate debt", "interest margin", "percentage spread" → use DebtInstrumentBasisSpreadOnVariableRate1.
